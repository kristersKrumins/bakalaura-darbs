About Us

Pilgrim Music is a dedicated violin shop located in the heart of the city. We pride ourselves on providing both budding musicians and seasoned professionals with the highest quality violins and accessories. Our team of experienced luthiers ensures that each instrument is meticulously crafted and tuned to perfection.

Established in 1990, our shop has been a haven for violinists, offering expert advice, repair services, and a wide range of violins, bows, and accessories.
